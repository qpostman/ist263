Quinn Postman Biography

Quinn Postman is a sophomore student at Syracuse University. He is majoring in Magazine, News & Digital Journalism at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications and Innovation, Society & Technology at School of Information Studies.

His hometown is Haworth, New Jersey. Quinn lives with his parents, Chad and Mary. He went to high school at Northern Valley Regional High School at Demarest. NVD is a collection of three towns: Haworth, Demarest, and Closter. Quinn played soccer and track & field in high school, winning a league championship with NVD Boys soccer and a state championship with NVD Track & Field. Quinn graduated high school in 2023.
